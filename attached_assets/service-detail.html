Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can a plumber arrive for emergency services?

For emergency plumbing services, we aim to have a professional at your location within 30-60 minutes, depending on your area and provider availability. Our platform prioritizes emergency requests to ensure quick response times.

Do your plumbers provide their own tools and materials?

Yes, all our plumbers come fully equipped with professional tools needed for the job. For materials and replacement parts, they can either use what they have on hand or purchase what's needed with your approval. The cost of materials will be clearly communicated before installation.

How are your service providers vetted?

All plumbers on our platform go through a rigorous verification process that includes background checks, skill assessment, certification verification, and in-person interviews. We also continuously monitor customer feedback to ensure ongoing quality of service.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely satisfied with the service provided, please contact our customer support within 7 days of service completion. We offer a service guarantee and will work to resolve any issues, which may include sending another professional to fix the problem at no additional cost.
